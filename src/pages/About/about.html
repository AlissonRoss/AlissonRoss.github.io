About Me
My name is Alisson Ross. I am a Computer Science and Engineering Major at Merced Community College. I am the Computational Alliance of Hispanic-Serving Institutions advocate and intern at Merced College. My goal is to become a Computer Science Professor to encourage first generation, underrepresented students to pursue or implement STEM into their studies. I am also interrested in WebAssembly and Progressive Web Applications I would love to help implement WebAssembly standards.
